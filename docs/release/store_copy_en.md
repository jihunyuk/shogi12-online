## Long Description (4000 chars max)

**Shogi12 Online** is a fast-paced mini strategy board game inspired by traditional Shogi.

### Features

- **4x3 compact board** — intense matches in minutes
- **30-second turn timer** — every move counts
- **Piece drops** — captured pieces become your own reinforcements
- **Entry Victory rule** — advance your 王 to the enemy's last row to win

### Game Modes

- **Local Match** — two players on the same device
- **VS Computer** — practice against an AI opponent
- **Online Multiplayer** — challenge players worldwide in real time

### Pieces

| Piece | Movement |
|-------|----------|
| 王 (King) | 1 square in any direction |
| 將 (General) | 1 square orthogonally |
| 相 (Bishop) | 1 square diagonally |
| 子 (Pawn) | 1 square forward |
| 後 (Promoted Pawn) | 5 directions (forward diagonals + orthogonal) |

### Win Conditions

1. Capture the opponent's 王
2. Advance your own 王 to the opponent's last row (Entry Victory)
